What is COBO Research Credit?

COBO is an experimental ERC-20 token deployed solely for education. Inside this lab, it functions as a COBO Research Credit (testnet only): a symbolic on-chain marker that a learning interaction was completed, documented, and verified.

Think of it as a digital lab notebook stamp, not a token with a price.

COBO is NOT:

  • not an investment or financial asset
  • not redeemable for anything
  • not for buying, selling, or trading
  • not an airdrop or reward
  • not affiliated with, endorsed by, or issued by LitVM
  • not connected to official token, roadmap, or governance rights

How to Reproduce

  1. Use a dedicated testnet wallet.
  2. Add LitVM LiteForge Testnet to MetaMask.
  3. Get a small amount of zkLTC testnet gas.
  4. Deploy or inspect an ERC-20 contract on testnet.
  5. Complete a small Ayni interaction.
  6. Complete a small swap.
  7. Save explorer hashes and notes.
